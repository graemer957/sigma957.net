I spend a decent amount of time running, but have always tried to avoid
listening to music or podcasts. Mainly so I can have my wits about me and enjoy
the environment around me. However, it's always felt like a miss when it comes
to listening to podcasts.

A colleague at work recently told me about bone-conducting headphones by a
company called [Shokz](https://uk.shokz.com/). He did warn me that the audio
quality was not great, but great is of course subjective. Having never heard of
them before, of course I did some research and they seemed like they would be worth
a punt. As a bonus they are approved by England Athletics.

* [PubNub with Stephen Blum](https://corrode.dev/podcast/s01e02-pubnub/)
